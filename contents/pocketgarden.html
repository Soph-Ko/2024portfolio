About Pocket Garden

Gardening is a branch of horticulture that includes planting and cultivating the desired plants in a certain space and breeding them. The process of learning the most basic economic activities as children grow up is similar to "gardening." Pocket Garden service aims to allow children to experience and enjoy the process of collecting money by growing various plants in a pocket garden.

Slogan

Taking inspiration from the transformation of 'H' into 'I' when rotated by 90 degrees, conveyed the meaning that children's dislike towards vegetables diminishes as they engage in gardening, fostering familiarity.

Visual Graphic

In the garden, various elements necessary for users to engage in gardening themselves also appear. To make the gardening elements that users can directly interact with stand out more, I designed the background with a monochromatic gray tone.

Background

Born and raised as the firstborn in a typical Korean family, a culture that values honesty and teamwork, she has become an open-minded, everyday team player to understand the lives of people. She is ready to work, respecting the opinions of different people.

Focus

She is a highly motivated and enthusiastic Product designer with a strong passion for brand, Generative and interaction design. She focuses on creating prototypes of high-quality execution across visual and interaction. These new media bring us a lot of new data and inputs we can really leverage.

Approach

Her approach to work is to look big. She prefers to design by opening up to unique and creative elements. To be creative, she makes sure to get new inputs everyday through continuous life exploration.

Prototype Motion Graphic Designer

As a Motion graphic designer, I independently designed scene-specific prototypes in line with the script, utilizing motion graphics and protopie. This project focused on creating scenes in the movie where characters use a dating app, making it appear as if the app is functioning in a realistic manner.

Somebody Logo

The heart shape in the dating app logo was specifically chosen to convey its romantic meaning.
Unlike a typical heart shape, it was designed to resemble a human heart, emphasizing a deeper connection. Moreover, the folded shapes within the heartbeats were strategically arranged to form the letter "S," symbolizing Somebody.

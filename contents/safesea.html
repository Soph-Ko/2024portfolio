As a UX UI designer, I was responsible for identified and analyzed the usability issues of the existing app service through usability testing. Based on the results, I created a new brand concept and designed an app service that aligns with this concept.
About Safe Sea app

An app by the Ministry of Oceans and Fisheries and is that Informs all sea related information
and real-time hazards for each sea characteristic in the East Sea, South Sea, and West Sea in South Korea.

About
I’m a multi-disciplinary product designer with over 3 years of experience, specializing in brand, generative, and interaction design. I focus on developing high-quality prototypes that seamlessly integrate visual aesthetics with interaction functionality.
Driven by a passion for innovation, I continuously explore new media, utilizing data and insights to push the boundaries of creativity and enhance user experiences.
My design philosophy is to think on a large scale and remain open to unique, creative solutions, drawing daily inspiration from continuous exploration and new experiences.
